Welcome to my site. I am Senior Research Associate: Data Management and Visualisation in Battery Research, within Energy Lancaster, Lancaster University, and the Faraday Institution.

Previously I have been working as a Dennis Sciama Postdoctoral Fellow at the Institute of Cosmology and Gravitation (ICG), a postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Computational Cosmology (ICC) (Department of Physics, Durham University) and at the Centre de physique des particules de Marseille.

I did my PhD under the supervision of Francisco J. Castander at the Institut de Ciencies de l'Espai (CSIC/IEEC). Besides schools, conferences and one observing run at Las Campanas observatory (Chile), during the years as PhD student, I enjoyed several long research visits at Max Plank Institute for Astrophysics (Germany), University of Florida (USA) and University of Durham (UK), plus a week at UCM (Spain).
